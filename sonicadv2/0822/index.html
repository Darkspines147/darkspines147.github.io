Sonic Adventure 2 Original Soundtrack Information!
We bring you information on the soundtracks for Sonic Adventure 2 & Sonic Adventure 2: Battle.
All volumes were made with the full cooperation of Sonic Team! They are packed with tons of images and setting materials, making them extremely voluminous. These six volumes are essential for mastering Sonic Adventure 2!
"Cuts Unleashed - SA2 Vocal Collection"
Publication：Marvelous Entertainment
Price:¥2,500 (excluding tax)
Release Date: Released on August 22, 2001

The vocal album contains all character vocal themes from the game and the main theme "Live and Learn", that was later released on the full soundtrack album of the game.
The hidden tracks on the disc contain instrumental tracks for the character themes and main theme.
Passion & Pride: Anthems with Attitude from the Sonic Adventure Era are two albums including characters' themes from Sonic Adventure and Sonic Adventure 2.
The Vox Collection contains thirteen tracks and the Instrumental Collection contains eleven tracks (the two missing tracks are the already instrumental themes, which are in the first album).
They are the modern equivilent of this disc.

"Multi-Dimensional - Sonic Adventure 2 Original Soundtrack"
Publication:Marvelous Entertainment
Price: ¥3,000 (excluding tax)
Release Date: Released on September 5, 2001

Multi-Dimensional contains all tracks from Sonic Adventure 2, but like Sonic Adventure, all the character themes are not
included in this album, since they were featured early on in the separated vocal album.
The only track from both albums featured is the main theme, "Live and Learn". All music tracks in the album are
composed by Jun Senoue, Kenichi Tokoi, Fumie Kumatani with Heigo Tani, and Tomoya Ohtani.
A digital version with two volumes simply titled Sonic Adventure 2 Original Sound Track was released digitally on 2014.
Vol. 1 has the tracks from disc 1 and a bonus instrumental track of "Escape from the City",
and Vol. 2 has the tracks from disc 2 and a bonus instrumental track of "Live and Learn".

A kind of "best of" soundtrack, featuring twenty-six songs. The modern equivilent is the 20th Anniversary Edition Soundtrack which adds four more songs for an even thirty.

"Sonic Adventure 2: Battle Strongest Strategy Guide"
Publication:Shogakukan
Price: ¥1,200 (excluding tax)
Release Date: Release date: March 1, 2002

The Strongest Strategy Guide covers most of the content of the Nintendo GameCube version of Sonic Adventure 2, including gameplay mechanics, enemies, levels, etc.

"Sonic Adventure 2: Battle Perfect Guide"
Publication：EnterBrain (Famitsu)
Price: ¥1,600 (excluding tax)
Release Date: Released on April 25, 2002

The Perfect Guide covers most of the content of the Nintendo GameCube version of Sonic Adventure 2, including gameplay mechanics, enemies, levels, etc.

"Sonic Advance & Sonic Adventure 2: Battle Official Perfect Guide"
Publication:Versus Books
Price: $14.99 + tax (CA$19.99 + tax)
Release Date: Released on Febuary 4, 2002

Sonic Advance & Sonic Adventure 2: Battle Official Perfect Guide is a double-packed strategy guidebook published by Versus Books in the United States for both Sonic Advance and Sonic Adventure 2: Battle.
If the reader reads from the front, they will read the Sonic Advance Official Perfect Guide. If the reader reads from the back, they will read the Sonic Adventure 2: Battle Official Perfect Guide.
