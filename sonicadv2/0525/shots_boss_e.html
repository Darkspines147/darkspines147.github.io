Shadow / SONIC THE HEDGEHOG
SHADOW THE HEDGEHOG

Introducing the hero side boss battle for Sonic.

Sonic has successfully fought his way through the military facilities on Prison Island. Now, amidst the thick forest Sonic has reached a safe haven or it may seem... Suddenly, Shadow appears out of nowhere. Shadow seems to be in hurry to get out of the forest, but why? Who is faster, who is stronger? Who will prevail?

Find your enemy's weakness and take advantage of it to be victorious! The formable enemy is a powerful opponent with abilities equal to those of our hero, Sonic.

Tails / Dr.EGGMAN
TAILS "MILES" PROWER w/ CYCLONE

Introducing the dark side boss battle for Eggman.

That cowardly fox boy "Tails" stands in the way of Eggman at Prison Island! "You will never defeat in a 100 years, you are no challenge for me!" Let the battle begin as both Eggman and Tails use this opportunity to fight each other armed with missiles.

Tails takes aims at Eggman with a Homing missile. Move right and left to evade the missile's attack! Now it's time to counterattack!
