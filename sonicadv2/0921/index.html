Sonic Adventure 2 Strategy Guide Information!
We bring you information on the strategy guide for Sonic Adventure 2.
Both volumes were made with the full cooperation of Sonic Team! They are packed with tons of images and setting materials, making them extremely voluminous. These two volumes are essential for mastering Sonic Adventure 2!
"Sonic Adventure 2 Hero/Dark Manual"
Publication：SoftBank Publishing
Price:¥1,400 yen (excluding tax)
Release Date: Released on July 6, 2001

Using maps, the book carefully explains the techniques and hints needed to complete both the Hero and Dark stories. It's also packed with advice on raising Chao!
This book is extremely useful for those who are still struggling to see the ending, or for those who struggle daily with raising Chao.

"Sonic Adventure 2 Perfect Guide"
Publication:Enterbrain (Famitsu)
Price: ¥1,800 yen (excluding tax)
Release Date: Released on October 29, 2001

Complete walkthroughs of every mission on every stage!
This is a truly "perfect" strategy guide, packed with complete data, including all emerald hints, hiding places for every small animal, and a complete Chao evolution chart.
Plus, it includes the "truth from 50 years ago" that wasn't revealed in the game! This is truly a must-have book for Sonic fans.

"Sonic Adventure 2: Prima's Official Strategy Guide"
Publication：Prima Games
Price: $14.99 (excluding tax)
Release Date: Released on June 20, 2001

The Prima's Official Strategy Guide covers most of the content of the Dreamcast version of Sonic Adventure 2, including gameplay mechanics, enemies, levels, etc.

"Sonic Adventure 2: Battle Strongest Strategy Guide"
Publication:Shogakukan
Price: ¥1,200 (excluding tax)
Release Date: Release date: March 1, 2002

The Strongest Strategy Guide covers most of the content of the Nintendo GameCube version of Sonic Adventure 2, including gameplay mechanics, enemies, levels, etc.

"Sonic Adventure 2: Battle Perfect Guide"
Publication：EnterBrain (Famitsu)
Price:¥1,400 yen (excluding tax)
Release Date: Released on April 25, 2002

The Perfect Guide covers most of the content of the Nintendo GameCube version of Sonic Adventure 2, including gameplay mechanics, enemies, levels, etc.

"Sonic Advance & Sonic Adventure 2: Battle Official Perfect Guide"
Publication:Versus Books
Price: $14.99 (excluding tax)
Release Date: Released on Febuary 4, 2002

Sonic Advance & Sonic Adventure 2: Battle Official Perfect Guide is a double-packed strategy guidebook published by Versus Books in the United States for both Sonic Advance and Sonic Adventure 2: Battle.
If the reader reads from the front, they will read the Sonic Advance Official Perfect Guide. If the reader reads from the back, they will read the Sonic Adventure 2: Battle Official Perfect Guide.
